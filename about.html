About Us

With a rich history in the automotive industry, Gemar Cars & CO has been a trusted dealership serving its community since 1998. For over 25 years, they've provided customers with a wide selection of vehicles and exceptional service.

Origins

Gemar Cars, founded in 1998, evolved from a racing team competing in the World Touring Car Championship to a luxury performance car dealership. Driven by a passion for speed and performance, the team consistently pushed limits on the track, attracting a loyal following of fans. After years of success, they brought their expertise to the streets, offering a curated selection of ultra-luxury vehicles that combine racing spirit with luxury comfort. Today, Gemar Cars is dedicated to delivering an unparalleled driving experience, with a team of knowledgeable experts helping discerning drivers find the perfect vehicle to match their style and preferences, backed by a commitment to excellence rooted in their racing heritage.

Our Founder

Gesit, a successful entrepreneur and car enthusiast, and Ammar, a young and ambitious driver, met at the Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps in 1995. Gesit was impressed by Ammar's skills behind the wheel and saw an opportunity to sign him as a driver for his newly formed racing team. As they talked, Gesit learned that Ammar was not only an exceptional driver but also a skilled mechanic and engineer, and he saw the potential for a strong partnership.

Over the next few years, Gesit and Ammar worked together to build their racing team, competing in prestigious events and slowly making a name for themselves. As their success on the track grew, so did their friendship. They shared a passion for performance, a commitment to excellence, and a desire to push the boundaries of what was possible. Their partnership became inseparable, both on and off the track.

In 1998, Gesit and Ammar decided to take their partnership to the next level by establishing Gemar Cars, a luxury performance car dealership that would bring their racing expertise to the streets. Today, Gemar Cars is a renowned brand, synonymous with high-performance driving and exceptional customer service. Gesit and Ammar's story is a testament to the power of partnership, passion, and perseverance, and their journey is an inspiration to anyone who has ever dared to dream big.
