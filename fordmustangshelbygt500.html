Description

The Ford Mustang Shelby GT500 is a high-performance variant of the Ford Mustang, a muscle car manufactured by Ford Motor Company. It is powered by a 5.2L Supercharged V8 engine, paired with a TREMEC 7-Speed DCT transmission. The car features a Rear-Wheel Drive system, with a 3.73 Axle Ratio and an Engine Oil Cooler. It also comes equipped with a 50-State Emissions System and a Regenerative Alternator. In terms of suspension, the GT500 features a Strut Front Suspension with Coil Springs, and a Multi-Link Rear Suspension with Coil Springs. It also has MagneRide Magnetic Fluid-Filled Shock Absorbers and an Automatic w/Driver Control Ride Control Sport Tuned Adaptive Suspension.

The car's exterior features a Clearcoat Paint finish, with a Body-Colored Front Bumper, Body-Colored Rear Bumper, and Black Side Windows Trim. It also has a Wing Spoiler, Front License Plate Bracket, and LED Brakelights. Inside, the GT500 has a range of features, including a 16 Gal. Fuel Tank, and a Tire Mobility Kit. It also has a range of safety features, including 4-Wheel Disc Brakes with 4-Wheel ABS, Front And Rear Vented Discs, Brake Assist, Hill Hold Control, and Electric Parking Brake. The GT500's dimensions include a Second Row Hip Room of 47.4 inches, a Trunk Space of 13.5 cubic feet, and a Turning Diameter / Radius, curb to curb of 43.8 feet.

Specifications

Engine type: 5.2L Supercharged V8, Rear-Wheel Drive, Cross plane crank,TREMEC 7-Speed DCT, 5,200 cc (4.0), 760 horsepower @ 7,300 rpm, 625 lb.-ft. @ 5,000 rpm.
