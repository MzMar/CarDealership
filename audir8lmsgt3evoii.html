Description

The Audi R8 LMS GT3 Evo II is a high-performance racing car designed for GT3 competition. It is based on the production Audi R8, but has been extensively modified to meet the demands of professional racing. The Evo II is the latest evolution of the R8 LMS, which has a successful history in GT3 racing, with multiple championships and wins in series such as the FIA GT World Challenge and the ADAC GT Masters. The car features a 5.2-liter V10 engine producing over 580 horsepower, paired with a six-speed sequential gearbox and rear-wheel drive.

The R8 LMS GT3 Evo II has undergone significant updates compared to its predecessor, including a new aerodynamic package, improved cooling systems, and enhanced safety features. The car's bodywork has been optimized to reduce drag and increase downforce, with new front and rear splitters, side skirts, and a rear wing. The interior has also been updated, with a new steering wheel, improved ergonomics, and enhanced data analysis systems. The result is a car that is both faster and more durable than its predecessor, making it an attractive option for professional racing teams and gentleman drivers alike.

Specifications

Engine type: 5.2-liter, V-10, direct fuel injection, six-speed sequential transmission, 5,204 cc, 585 HP (433 kW) at 8,250 rpm, 406 LB-FT (550 Nm) at 6,500 rpm.
